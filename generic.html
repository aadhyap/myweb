Hi my name is Aadhya and I am an upcoming graduate from Worcester Polytechnic Institute majoring in Computer Science!

I first started coding by attending a MIT hackathon in highschool! Ever since then I attended more and ended up winning one! I then was so in love with the idea of students prototyping and the community of entrepreneurs that I helped organize a hackathon in Boston, MA called MA Hacks. I then had the oppurtunity to participate in MIT Beaverworks where I was first introduced to drones and computer vision which I didn't know until now that this was my passion.

In the summers I would work at Raytheon BBN technologies and then MIT Lincoln Labratories. Throughout my time at WPI I developed a passion throughout my Java application projects, prototyping, and web development and design. Although, I recently found my focus and my niche that I know I will be pursuing further work on.

I continue to push myself everyday in that class as it is related to my Bimodal Quadruped project where I have to create an object tracking method and use SLAM.
